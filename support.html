Ecology + Reforestation €30K
The entire land was fully burned down in 2017, destroying a big part of the native trees. Now what is growing back is basically 1 species, mimosas. A non native invasive tree that grows fast and takes the sunlight for other plants. This makes it in the long term very hard to create a healthy diverse forest life and will have much higher changes for wild fires again in the future. Forest fires are a big challenge in Portugal but we can help to prevent them. We will work with ecologists to analyse the land, create a landscape plan. For years to come.

⭐️ Reward: Help prevent forest fire, offset your footprint and we name the the forest after you
Upgrade our energy supply €50K
Currently we are running fully off-grid on our own solar power. (4kw) Its awesome. That said we still need to use a generator for our big machines (3phase). If we want to be really offgrid, run machines, and scale up the community we need to setup ourselves with a better energy infrastructure.  In the coming years we will scale up with four times the amount of people but are simply not equipped for it. (we are also up for collaborations)

⭐️ Reward: Named in the video and you can come here anytime to charge your electric car.
Hook us up to solar - €100K
Running 100% on renewable energy is a long term goal for us. Easy to achieve for a small household, difficult to achieve with 380V machines like welding and a metal lathe. Is requires a big battery pack, solar panels and a backup like hydro/wind energy for the days without sun. It’s mainly a long term goal because it would take us a while before we have the funds. You can speed this up by providing the capital or sponsoring it.
